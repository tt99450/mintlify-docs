## Tips for Success

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Test your audio early" icon="microphone">
    Run the audio check before class so you don't miss the first few minutes troubleshooting.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Use a headset" icon="headphones">
    A headset reduces echo and helps everyone hear you clearly.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Raise your hand" icon="hand">
    It's the easiest way to get your teacher's attention without interrupting.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Check accessibility options" icon="universal-access">
    Closed captions, keyboard navigation, and other features are built in. See [Accessibility](/getting-started/accessibility).
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
