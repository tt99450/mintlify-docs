---

To view past Conferences, go to the Completed section in the Conferences panel.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 04 15 At 10 55 26 AM](/images/Screenshot-2026-04-15-at-10.55.26-AM.png)
</Frame>

If you did not record your session, there will be no recording or statistic information.

## Viewing past recordings and statistics

<Frame>
  ![Completedconfs](/images/completedconfs.png)
</Frame>

To view the recording, select **Play \[1\]**. To save the video recording, **right-click** on the video player and select **"Save video as".**

To view the Learning Analytics Dashboard of the session, select **Statistics \[2\].**
