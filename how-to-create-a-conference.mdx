title: "How to Create a Conference"
---

To create a Conference in Schoology, you can go to **Conferences \[1\]** in the navigation panel and select **Create New Conference \[2\]**.

<Frame>
  ![Createaconference](/images/createaconference.png)
</Frame>

## Creating a Conference

<Frame>
  ![Createnew](/images/createnew.png)
</Frame>

You can add the **name of the Conference \[1\]** and the **start date and time \[2\]** of the conference. You can also add the end time of the conference as an option.

Select **Create \[3\]** to add your conference.
