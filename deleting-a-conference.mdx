## Deleting a conference

To delete any active or past conferences, go to the gear icon next to the conference and select **Delete**.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 04 15 At 10 52 25 AM](/images/Screenshot-2026-04-15-at-10.52.25-AM.png)
</Frame>

You will be prompted to confirm your deletion.
