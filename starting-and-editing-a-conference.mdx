## Starting and Editing a Conference

<Frame>
  ![Startconference](/images/startconference.png)
</Frame>

After creating a Conference, you can select **Start Conference \[1\]** to begin the BigBlueButton session. This will automatically make you join the session in a new tab.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 04 15 At 11 05 20 AM](/images/Screenshot-2026-04-15-at-11.05.20-AM-1.png)
</Frame>

By selecting the gear icon next to the conference of your choosing, you can **edit \[2\]** or **delete \[3\]** your conference before starting it.

<Frame>
  ![Editconference](/images/editconference.png)
</Frame>

You can edit the title, start and end time details of the conference.

## Rejoining a Conference

When a conference is started, you can join by selecting the link of the conference that is in progress.

<Frame>
  ![Joinconference](/images/joinconference.png)
</Frame>

Conferences that can be joined will be located in the active panel by default.
