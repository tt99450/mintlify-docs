---
title: "Move webcam(s) in a live session"
description: "Any user can drag webcams to a preferred position in their own view of the BigBlueButton interface."
---

Any user can drag webcams to a preferred position within their own BigBlueButton interface. By default, webcams display at the top or bottom middle of the layout.

![Webcams positioned in the BigBlueButton interface](/images/360031528691.png)

You can drag and drop a single webcam anywhere on your screen, or snap webcams to the top or bottom of the presentation area.

For a walkthrough, see this [overview video](https://youtu.be/xeLOzKBUdIk?si=yfuJRot4zY-sVz9j&t=120).

<Note>
  Moving webcams only changes the local view for each user. A moderator cannot push a webcam layout out to everyone.
</Note>
