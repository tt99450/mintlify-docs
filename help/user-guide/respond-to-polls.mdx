Presenters in BigBlueButton can initiate polls throughout the live session. Connected users can reply when prompted and contribute to the poll.

When a poll has been initiated, all users will see the option to respond on their screen.

## Responding to Polls

<Frame>
  ![Pollprompt](/images/pollprompt.png)
</Frame>

Polling options will appear on your screen. Depending on the device you are using and the screen layout (portrait or landscape), the options may appear in the **bottom right** of the BigBlueButton interface.

<Frame>
  ![Pollpop](/images/pollpop.png)
</Frame>

Select your chosen reply to lock in your polling answer; the polling options will vary depending on the presenter's content.

Only the presenter can see the incoming responses and can publish the anonymized results to the presentation screen.

<Frame>
  ![Pollresultspres](/images/pollresultspres.png)
</Frame>

Once the presenter has published the results, users will no longer see the option to reply.
