---

When a presenter starts a poll or quiz during the session, any user can respond. The presenter sees incoming responses and can publish the anonymized results to the slide.

## Respond to a poll

When a poll is started, response options appear on your screen.

<Frame>
  ![Pollpop](/images/pollpop.png)
</Frame>

Depending on your device and screen layout (portrait or landscape), the options may appear in the **bottom-right** of the BigBlueButton interface.

<Frame>
  ![Pollprompt](/images/pollprompt.png)
</Frame>

Select your answer to lock it in. The available options vary based on the presenter's question.

## Respond to a quiz

A quiz prompt appears the same way as a poll, in the lower-right corner.

<Frame>
  ![Quizpop](/images/quizpop.png)
</Frame>

Select your answer to submit it.

## See published results

Only the presenter sees incoming responses. Once the presenter publishes the anonymized results, they appear on the presentation slide and the response prompt disappears for all users.

  ![Pollresultspres](/images/pollresultspres.png)
</Frame>
