---
title: "Using away/available mode"
description: "Toggle between available and away to mute your mic, mask your webcam, and signal your status to others in the session."
---

Any user can toggle between **available** and **away** to mute their microphone, mask their webcam, and signal their status to the rest of the session.

When you enter a session, your status is set to **available** by default in the right-hand options menu.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 16 At 2 29 06 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-16at2.29.06PM.png)
</Frame>

To set yourself as away, toggle the switch.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 16 At 2 30 18 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-16at2.30.18PM.png)
</Frame>

When you set yourself as away, BigBlueButton:

- Mutes your microphone
- Masks your webcam
- Adds an "away" clock icon to your user avatar
- Posts a note in the chat that you are away

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 16 At 2 31 12 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-16at2.31.12PM.png)
</Frame>

To return to **available**, toggle the switch back. All four away effects are reversed.
