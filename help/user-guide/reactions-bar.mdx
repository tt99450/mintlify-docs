The Reactions Bar makes it much easier for students to respond with emojis to the educator. The emoji is displayed in the user avatar area for 1 minute.

To activate the reactions bar, press the emoji icon.

![Reactions Bar icon](/images/33396573869581.png)

There are 5 emojis to choose from in the Reactions Bar:

![Reactions Bar opened showing five emoji options](/images/33396597606157.png)

Select an emoji to display it in your avatar beside your name in the user list.

![Emoji displayed in user avatar](/images/19462808885261.png)

The emoji will be displayed for 1 minute or until it is unselected.
