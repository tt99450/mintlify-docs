---
title: "Reactions Bar"
description: "Any user can send a quick emoji reaction that appears next to their name in the user list for one minute."
---

Any user can send a quick emoji reaction to respond to the presenter without interrupting. The reaction shows next to your name in the user list for one minute, or until you clear it.

To open the Reactions Bar, select the emoji icon.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 16 At 7 15 52 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-16at7.15.52PM.png)
</Frame>

The bar shows five emojis to choose from.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 16 At 7 13 05 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-16at7.13.05PM.png)
</Frame>

Select an emoji to display it next to your name in the user list.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 16 At 7 17 45 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-16at7.17.45PM.png)
</Frame>

The emoji clears automatically after one minute, or as soon as you select it again.
