---

You can adjust the ratio of webcams to presentation area (which may also be showing a screen share).

To adjust, hover your cursor over the border between the presentation area and the webcam(s) until a resize icon appears.

Drag the cursor down to enlarge the webcam(s)/reduce the presentation area.

![adjustwebcams.gif](/images/adjustwebcams.gif)

This change is local to your client -- it does not change how other viewers are watching the webcam.
