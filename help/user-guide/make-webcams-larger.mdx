---
title: "Make webcams larger"
description: "Any user can resize the ratio between the webcam strip and the presentation area in their own view."
---

Any user can adjust the ratio between webcams and the presentation area (which may also be showing a screen share). The change applies only to your own view — it does not affect what other users see.

To resize, hover over the border between the presentation area and the webcam strip until a resize cursor appears, then drag down to enlarge the webcams and shrink the presentation area.
