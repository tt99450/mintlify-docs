---
title: "Raise Hand"
description: "Any user can raise or lower their hand to signal the presenter without interrupting the session."
---

Any user can raise their hand to signal the presenter — for example, to ask a question or request to speak. Lowering your hand removes the signal.

To raise your hand, select the hand icon.

<Frame>
  ![Raisehand](/images/raisehand.png)
</Frame>

A hand emoji appears on your user icon.

<Frame>
  ![Raisehandlist](/images/raisehandlist.png)
</Frame>

A prompt also appears in the top-right corner confirming that your hand is raised.

<Frame>
  ![Raisehandprompt](/images/raisehandprompt.png)
</Frame>

When you lower your hand, the emoji disappears from your user icon and another prompt confirms the change.

<Frame>
  ![Lowerhandprompt](/images/lowerhandprompt.png)
</Frame>
