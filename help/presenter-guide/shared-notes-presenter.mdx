# Use shared notes

In addition to public and private chats, all users in BigBlueButton can access the Shared Notes panel.

Shared Notes enable all the users in a meeting to contribute to a common text-based file; you can use formatting options and always export the Shared Notes later.

Located under the notes section, select **Shared Notes [1]** to expand the notes panel. **Formatting Options [2]** such as Bold, Italic, and undo/redo can be found at the top of the notes panel. To find undo/redo and the download buttons, press the **'+' [3]** button.

To download the current notes, select the **Export [4]** button.

A list of available formats will appear. Select the preferred format and save to your device.

## Exporting notes to the whiteboard

To export shared notes to the board, select the **Notes options** menu in the upper right-hand corner.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 16 At 6 56 13 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-16at6.56.13PM.png)
</Frame>

If you want to pin the shared notes to the presentation area, select Pin notes onto whiteboard.

<Frame>
  ![Pinnoteswhite](/images/pinnoteswhite.png)
</Frame>

You and every other user in the session will be able to type in the shared notes.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 16 At 6 52 27 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-16at6.52.27PM.png)
</Frame>

To unpin the shared notes back into the sidebar, press the X button on the right-hand corner.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 16 At 7 08 18 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-16at7.08.18PM.png)
</Frame>

Select **Convert notes to presentation** to post the notes onto the presentation slide as a pdf.

<Frame>
  ![Convertnotes](/images/convertnotes.png)
</Frame>

The shared notes that were typed in the session will appear like a document.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 16 At 6 53 05 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-16at6.53.05PM.png)
</Frame>

## Important notes

- Shared Notes are not captured in the recording processing.
- All users must open their Shared Notes pad individually.
- Shared Notes contributions are anonymous.
