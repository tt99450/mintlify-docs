Anyone in a BigBlueButton session can [contribute to Shared Notes](#contribute-to-shared-notes), [export the notes](#export-the-notes-to-a-file) to a file, or — as a presenter — [pin or convert the notes](#pin-or-convert-notes-to-the-whiteboard) onto the whiteboard.

To open the notes panel, select **Shared Notes** **[1]** in the left sidebar. **Formatting Options** **[2]** such as **Bold** and **Italic** appear at the top of the panel. To reveal undo, redo, and download buttons, select the **'+'** **[3]** button.

When another user edits the notes, an alert appears on the **Shared Notes** icon.

## Pin or convert notes to the whiteboard

As presenter, you can pin the live notes onto the presentation area or convert them into a slide.

To open the notes options, select the **Notes options** menu in the upper-right corner.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 16 At 6 56 13 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-16at6.56.13PM.png)
</Frame>

### Pin notes onto the whiteboard

To pin the notes to the presentation area, select **Pin notes onto whiteboard**. Every user in the session can continue typing in the pinned notes.

<Frame>
  ![Pinnoteswhite](/images/pinnoteswhite.png)
</Frame>

To unpin and return the notes to the sidebar, select the **X** in the upper-right corner.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 16 At 7 08 18 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-16at7.08.18PM.png)
</Frame>

### Convert notes to a presentation slide

To post the notes onto the presentation as a PDF slide, select **Convert notes to presentation**.

<Frame>
  ![Convertnotes](/images/convertnotes.png)
</Frame>

The notes appear in the presentation area as a document.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 16 At 6 53 05 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-16at6.53.05PM.png)
</Frame>

<Note>
  - Shared Notes are not captured in the recording.
  - Each user must open their own Shared Notes panel.
  - Contributions to Shared Notes are anonymous.
</Note>
