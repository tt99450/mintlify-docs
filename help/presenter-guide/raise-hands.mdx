---
title: "Raise Hands"
description: "Users raise their hand to signal the presenter, and presenters can lower individual hands or all hands at once."
---

Any user can raise their hand to signal the presenter — for example, to ask a question or request to speak. Presenters can lower a single hand or lower all hands at once.

To raise your hand, select the hand icon.

## Lower another user's hand

As a presenter, select a user's name in the user list to lower that one hand, or select **Lower all hands** to lower every raised hand at once.
