## Upload documents and enable them for download

In order to upload documents and enable them for download, you must first have the presenter role. By default, the first **Moderator** to enter the BigBlueButton session is allocated presenter role capabilities; if you are a viewer, you will need to request presenter status from the moderator.

![Screen_Shot_2019-03-11_at_2.18.28_PM.png](images/Screen_Shot_2019-03-11_at_2.18.28_PM.png)

When a user has the presenter role, the **presenter icon** will appear over their avatar in the users list.

## Verify document size and format

Maximum size is **30 MB or 150 pages** per document; you can upload multiple documents into BigBlueButton as long as they are under the size restrictions.

BigBlueButton will accept Office documents (**.doc .docx .pptx**), text documents **(.txt)**, images **(.png, .jpg)** and **Adobe Acrobat documents (.pdf)**; we recommend converting documents to **.pdf** prior to uploading for best results.

## Upload a presentation

![mceclip0.png](/images/mceclip0_13.png)

Select the **Actions menu [1]** in the lower left-hand corner of the presentation window, then choose **Manage presentations [2]**.

![Screen_Shot_2019-05-01_at_4.14.05_PM.png](/images/Screen_Shot_2019-05-01_at_4.14.05_PM.png)

The BigBlueButton presentation uploader will appear. The uploader allows you to **drag and drop [3]** files into the uploader or **browse for files [4]** on your OS.

![Screen_Shot_2019-05-01_at_4.19.34_PM.png](/images/Screen_Shot_2019-05-01_at_4.19.34_PM.png)

When uploading multiple files at once, be sure to set the file you'd like to be displayed by default, then check the **green circle** to the right of the document.

![Screen_Shot_2019-05-01_at_4.21.21_PM.png](/images/Screen_Shot_2019-05-01_at_4.21.21_PM.png)

Once you have selected your chosen file(s) for upload, press **start** to convert and upload the files.

Depending on the size of your document, it may take several moments for it to convert and finally upload to the meeting.

## Enable for download

Any presenter uploading a document can enable it for download by viewers of the live conference at any time.

![Screen_Shot_2019-05-01_at_4.19.42_PM.png](/images/Screen_Shot_2019-05-01_at_4.19.42_PM.png)

To enable, select the **enable download icon** located to the right of the document.

![Screen_Shot_2019-05-01_at_4.19.59_PM.png](/images/Screen_Shot_2019-05-01_at_4.19.59_PM.png)

The icon will change to green to indicate the document selected can be downloaded by viewers.

## What viewers see

![Screen_Shot_2019-03-11_at_3.09.41_PM.png](/images/Screen_Shot_2019-03-11_at_3.09.41_PM.png)

Viewers will be able to download the enabled documents via the file **download icon** in the bottom left of their screens.

## Switch between presentations

Once you have uploaded all of your presentations, you can then switch between them on the fly.

![mceclip0.png](/images/mceclip0_14.png)

Select the **Actions menu [1]** in the lower left-hand corner of the presentation window, then choose a **presentation from one of the already uploaded options [2]**.
