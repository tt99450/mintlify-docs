## A presenter in BigBlueButton has the ability to share an external audio/video with users in the live session.

The presenter role enables the assigned user the ability to share and control the video playback from an external video.

Using the **Share an external video** command, you can share a URL for

- YouTube
- Vimeo
- Instructure Media (previously called Arc)
- Twitch and Daily Motio
- Panopto
- Direct link to a .mp4 file, as in [https://ubuntu.bigbluebutton.org/test/FredDixon.mp4](http://ubuntu.bigbluebutton.org/test/FredDixon.mp4)
- Direct link to a .mp3 file, as in [https://ubuntu.bigbluebutton.org/test/audioSample.mp3](http://ubuntu.bigbluebutton.org/test/audioSample.mp3)
- Direct link to a .mp4/.mp3 file shared in DropBox (get a free dropbox account at [dropbox.com/basic](https://www.dropbox.com/basic) to upload your videos).

<Warning>
  When using this feature the video will not appear in the processed recordings.
</Warning>

1. Select the **'+'** menu as presenter and choose **Share an external video**.

2. A dialog box appears where you can paste in URL to your video and select **Share a new video**.

![Image](/images/image-3.png)

When you select Share a new video, the video will appear in the presentare area.  You can then start playing the video and BigBlueButton will synchronize the playback for all users.  At anytime you can:

1. Start/Pause the video
2. Move the play head
3. Stop sharing

3. To stop sharing, select the 'X' in the upper right-hand corner of the presentation area.

![Image](/images/image-4.png)

When you stop sharing, the presentation area will revert to the previous presentation.
