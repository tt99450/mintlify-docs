## Share an external audio/video with users in the live session

The presenter role enables the assigned user to share and control video playback from an external video.

Using the **Share an external video** command, you can share a URL for

- YouTube
- Vimeo
- Instructure Media (previously called Arc)
- Twitch and Dailymotion
- Panopto
- Direct link to a .mp4 file, as in [https://ubuntu.bigbluebutton.org/test/FredDixon.mp4](http://ubuntu.bigbluebutton.org/test/FredDixon.mp4)
- Direct link to a .mp3 file, as in [https://ubuntu.bigbluebutton.org/test/audioSample.mp3](http://ubuntu.bigbluebutton.org/test/audioSample.mp3)
- Direct link to a .mp4/.mp3 file shared in Dropbox (get a free Dropbox account at [dropbox.com/basic](https://www.dropbox.com/basic) to upload your videos).

<Warning>
  When using this feature, the video will not appear in the processed recordings.
</Warning>

Select the **'+' Action menu [1]** and choose **Share an external video [2]**.

<Frame>
  ![Externalvideo](/images/externalvideo.png)
</Frame>

A dialog box appears where you can paste in the URL to your video and select **Share a new video**.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 16 At 4 24 08 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-16at4.24.08PM.png)
</Frame>

When you select **Share a new video**, the video will appear in the presentation area. You can then start playing the video and BigBlueButton will synchronize the playback for all users. At any time you can:

- Start/Pause the video
- Move the playhead
- Stop sharing

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 16 At 4 17 22 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-16at4.17.22PM.png)
</Frame>

To stop sharing, select the **X** in the upper right-hand corner of the presentation area.

<Frame>
  ![Stopsharing](/images/stopsharing.png)
</Frame>

When you stop sharing, the presentation area will revert to the previous presentation.
