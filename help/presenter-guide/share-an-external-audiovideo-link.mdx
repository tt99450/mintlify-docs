---
title: "Share an External Audio/Video Link"
description: "Presenters can share an external audio or video URL with everyone in the session and control playback for all users."
---

Presenters can share an external audio or video URL with everyone in the session. BigBlueButton synchronizes playback for all users so the presenter can [start the video](#start-sharing-a-video), pause it, scrub the playhead, and [stop sharing](#stop-sharing-the-video) when done.

BigBlueButton supports URLs from:

- YouTube
- Vimeo
- Instructure Media (previously called Arc)
- Twitch and Dailymotion
- Panopto
- A direct link to an `.mp4` file, for example [https://ubuntu.bigbluebutton.org/test/FredDixon.mp4](http://ubuntu.bigbluebutton.org/test/FredDixon.mp4)
- A direct link to an `.mp3` file, for example [https://ubuntu.bigbluebutton.org/test/audioSample.mp3](http://ubuntu.bigbluebutton.org/test/audioSample.mp3)
- A direct link to an `.mp4` or `.mp3` file shared in Dropbox (get a free Dropbox account at [dropbox.com/basic](https://www.dropbox.com/basic) to upload your videos)

<Warning>
  Externally shared videos do not appear in the processed recordings.
</Warning>

## Start sharing a video

To share a video, select the **'+' Actions menu** **[1]**, then **Share an external video** **[2]**.

<Frame>
  ![Externalvideo](/images/externalvideo.png)
</Frame>

In the dialog box, paste your video URL and select **Share a new video**.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 16 At 4 24 08 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-16at4.24.08PM.png)
</Frame>

The video appears in the presentation area, and BigBlueButton synchronizes playback for everyone in the session. As presenter, you can:

- Start or pause the video
- Move the playhead
- Stop sharing

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 16 At 4 17 22 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-16at4.17.22PM.png)
</Frame>

## Stop sharing the video

To stop sharing, select the **X** in the upper-right corner of the presentation area.

<Frame>
  ![Stopsharing](/images/stopsharing.png)
</Frame>

The presentation area reverts to the previous presentation.
