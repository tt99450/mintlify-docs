## Polling

As a presenter, you can use polling to engage students, perform test/exam reviews or pop quizzes, or use polling informally to collect information about your users.

By default, the first **Moderator** to enter the BigBlueButton session is allocated the presenter role capabilities; if you are a viewer, you will need to request presenter status from the moderator.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 17 At 1 34 30 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-17at1.34.30PM-2.png)
</Frame>

When a user has the presenter role, the **presenter icon** will appear over their avatar in the users list.

## Start a poll

<Frame>
  ![Findpolling](/images/findpolling.png)
</Frame>

Select the **Actions menu** **\[1\]** in the lower left-hand corner of the presentation window, then **Start a poll/quiz** **\[2\]**.

The Poll option is selected by default. The remainder of this section will discuss polls, and the next section will discuss quizzes.

<Frame>
  ![Pollingmenu](/images/pollingmenu.png)
</Frame>

The panel will open with several options. You can also enter a question for the poll in the **Write your question** **\[3\]** section. Select from the **Response Types** **\[4\]** options to initiate a poll.

For example, if you write **"Did Einstein discover the atom?"** **\[1\]**, select **True / False** **\[2\]**, and select **Start Poll** **\[3\]**, it will initiate the poll for all users.

![](/images/38803805619213.png)

When Start Poll is selected, BigBlueButton will poll all users.

![](/images/38803805619341.png)

As users respond, such as clicking False, the presenter will see the live results.

![](/images/38803805620877.png)

Here the poll/quiz panel shows the **False** **\[1\]** result from the student. Note: Selecting **Anonymous Poll** will not show the student's name for each result.

Selecting **Publish results** **\[2\]** will both publish the results to the chat:

<Frame>
  ![Pollresults](/images/pollresults-2.png)
</Frame>

and to the slide.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 24 At 12 21 54 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-24at12.21.54PM.png)
</Frame>

The published poll results can be moved, resized, deleted, or duplicated on the whiteboard.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 16 At 3 51 58 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-16at3.51.58PM-1.png)
</Frame>

Published results appear in recordings both in the chat and slide as well.

## Quizzing

BigBlueButton now supports quizzing, which you can think of as polls with a correct answer.

To do a quiz, select **Quiz** **\[1\]** and the options now show response types with multiple choices.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 24 At 12 09 52 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-24at12.09.52PM.png)
</Frame>

After selecting a Quiz, you can enter a question, such as **"Are insects animals?"** **\[1\]**, choose **Yes / No / Absention** **\[2\]**, select **True \[3\]** as the correct answer, and select **Start quiz** **\[4\]**.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 24 At 12 13 40 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-24at12.13.40PM.png)
</Frame>

After you select Start Quiz, users will see a prompt to respond to the quiz.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 16 At 3 32 27 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-16at3.32.27PM.png)
</Frame>

As before, the presenter will see the live results. Since it's a quiz with a correct answer, BigBlueButton will indicate if the student selected the correct answer.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 17 At 1 48 47 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-17at1.48.47PM.png)
</Frame>

When publishing the results, it will also show the correct answer unless you **unselect** Display correct answers to all participants.

Here is what the published results look like in the chat with the correct result indicated.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 16 At 3 33 04 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-16at3.33.04PM.png)
</Frame>
