---
title: "How do I move shared notes to the whiteboard?"
description: "Instructors can move the shared notes to the whiteboard presentation as presentation. "
---

Instructors can move the shared notes to the whiteboard presentation as presentation. To do this, select the three dots in the top right corner of the **Shared Notes **window and select **Convert notes to presentation**. The Shared Notes will then be converted and displayed in the presentation area.

![mceclip37.png](/images/19159127214989.png)

The Shared Notes will then be available as a presentation.

![mceclip39.png](/images/19159127216653.png)

Which can then be marked up on the whiteboard.

![mceclip38.png](/images/19159170073485.png)

Instructors can also pin the shared notes to the whiteboard presentation area for further collaborative work among students. To do this, select the three dots in the top right corner of the Shared Notes window and select Pin notes onto whiteboard.

![mceclip2.png](/images/19159127218445.png)

To unpin the shared notes, press the X button on the top right-hand side of the whiteboard area.

![2.6\_-\_Pin_Shared_Notes_to_Whiteboard.gif](/images/19159127219469.gif)
