You can now disable the self-view of your own webcam. This will reduce the fatigue of viewing

your own image, often people focus on their appearance over the content being shared.

## Disable self-view

1. Click on the carrot beside your name

2. Select Disable self-view

![](/images/19427950103181.png)

When you disable the view you will see an image of yourself with an overlay reminding you that **your webcam is still active and others see you.**

![](/images/19428020859789.png)

## Enable self-view

1. Click on the carrot beside your name

2. Select Enable self-view

![](/images/19428271034893.png)
