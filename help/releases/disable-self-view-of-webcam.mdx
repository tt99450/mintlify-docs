Any user sharing a webcam can hide their own self-view to reduce the fatigue of watching themselves on camera. Disabling self-view affects only your own display — your webcam continues to broadcast to other users.

## Disable self-view

To disable self-view:

1. Select the **caret** next to your name.
2. Select **Disable self-view**.

<Frame>
  ![Diview](/images/diview.png)
</Frame>

A placeholder image of yourself appears with an overlay reminding you that **your webcam is still active and others see you**.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 31 At 9 50 42 AM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-31at9.50.42AM.png)
</Frame>

## Enable self-view

To turn self-view back on:

1. Select the **caret** next to your name.
2. Select **Enable self-view**.
