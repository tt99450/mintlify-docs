The ability to add a countdown (time) or count up (stopwatch) is displayed to all participants in the session. An audible alarm is played when the timer elapses.

To activate a timer or stopwatch:

1. Select the action menu
2. Select Activate timer/stopwatch

<Frame>
  ![Timerstop](/images/timerstop.png)
</Frame>

The side panel will update and show you the timer/stopwatch options.
