Presenters can display a [countdown timer](#timer) or [count-up stopwatch](#stopwatch) to all participants in the session. An audible alarm plays when a timer elapses.

## Activate a timer or stopwatch

1. Select the **action menu**.
2. Select **Activate timer/stopwatch**.

<Frame>
  ![Timerstop](/images/timerstop.png)
</Frame>

The side panel updates to show the timer and stopwatch options.
