title: "Download of Original Presentation"
description: "You can now download the original version of the presentation as well as the current version including all annotations made during the session."
---

You can now download the original version of the presentation, as well as the current version, including all annotations made during the session.

You can enable the ability to download the original presentation, from the **Upload/Manage Presentations** window, by selecting the three-dot hamburger beside the presentation name and selecting **Enable download of the presentation**.

![](/images/19431462640653.png)

In the main presentation area, you will see a down arrow in the bottom left-hand corner. Clicking on this icon will download a copy of the original presentation.

![](/images/19431705886477.png)
