Presenters in hybrid classes can share a webcam as the main presentation content. Use this to display a physical whiteboard, focus on a particular student, or highlight an area of the classroom.

<Note>
  The camera being shared as content cannot already be in use as your main webcam.
</Note>

## Share a camera as content

To share a camera as the main content:

1. Select the **action button**.
2. Select **Share camera as content**.

<Frame>
  ![Camascontent](/images/camascontent.png)
</Frame>

The webcam settings dialog appears. Select an available webcam.

<Frame>
  ![Webcam settings dialog](/images/Screenshot2026-03-24at9.06.54AM.png)
</Frame>

The selected camera appears in the presentation area for everyone in the session.

<Frame>
  ![Camera shown in the presentation area](/images/camcontent.png)
</Frame>

## Stop sharing the camera as content

1. Select the **action button**.
2. Select **Stop camera as content**.

<Frame>
  ![Stopcontent](/images/stopcontent.png)
</Frame>
