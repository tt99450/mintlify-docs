In hybrid classes, there is often a need to display a physical whiteboard or focus on a particular student, or area of the classroom. You can now do this via a webcam shared to the main content area.

\* Note the camera being shared can not be in use (as your main webcam) at the time of selection

## To share the camera as content

1. Select the action button
2. Select Share camera as content

<Frame>
  ![Camascontent](/images/camascontent.png)
</Frame>

You will then be presented with the Webcam settings model, where you will be able to select a webcam (as long as it is not in use already)

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 24 At 9 06 54 AM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-24at9.06.54AM.png)
</Frame>

Your camera will appear on the presentation area:

<Frame>
  ![Camcontent](/images/camcontent.png)
</Frame>

## To stop sharing the camera as content

1. Select the action button
2. Select Stop camera as content

<Frame>
  ![Stopcontent](/images/stopcontent.png)
