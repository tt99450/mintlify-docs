The Reactions Bar makes it much easier for students to respond with emojis to the

educator. The emoji is displayed in the user avatar area for 1 minute.

To activate the reactions bar, press the emoji icon.

![reactionsbar.png](/images/33396573869581)

There are 5 emojis to choose from in the reaction bar:

![reactbaropened.png](/images/33396597606157)

You can activate the emoji you want shown in the avatar beside your name in the user list.

![](/images/19462808885261)

The emoji will be displayed for 1 minute or until it is unselected.
