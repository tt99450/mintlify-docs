---
title: "Why do I not see my webcam in the recording?"
---

If you share your screen, the screen share takes over the entire playback area, as shown below (no webcams are visible).

![mceclip1.png](/images/mceclip1_4.png)

If your entire presentation is screen share, then no webcams will be visible during the playback.

If you intend to share your screen and create a recording, and want users to see your webcam, we recommend starting the recording a few minutes before you start screen sharing.  That way, the webcam will be visible for the first few minutes until screen sharing starts.
