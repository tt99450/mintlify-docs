---
title: "User is getting disconnected"
---

See [minimum bandwidth requirements](/help/faq/what-are-the-minimum-requirements-for-bigbluebutton) for BigBlueButton.

If a user's connection is not sufficient for the BigBlueButton session, the client may take a long time to load or, after a period of time, disconnect (it may look like to users they got kicked out).

The biggest use of bandwidth is webcams.  If a session is sharing lots of a webcams, a user can elect to turn off the webcams on their client to (significantly) reduce the bandwidth requirements.

To turn off webcams, click the **Settings** menu

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image.png)
</Frame>

And choose **Data savings** and turn off webcams:
