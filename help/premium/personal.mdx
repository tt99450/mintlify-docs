Blindside Networks offers [my.bigbluebutton.com](https://my.bigbluebutton.com/) to let you instantly try BigBlueButton or setup a personal account that gives you your own personal room to invite others.

  * **Instant** - Launch a meeting immediately, with no sign-up. Useful for trying BigBlueButton or for a quick ad-hoc call. The meeting ends when the time limit is reached.
  * **Personal** - Create a free account with your email, Google, or Microsoft sign-in. You get a permanent personal room with a shareable link you can reuse for every session.

The following table shows the differences

| Feature             | Instant     | Personal              |
| ------------------- | ----------- | --------------------- |
| Account required    | No          | Yes (free)            |
| Personal room       | -           | Yes                   |
| Shareable room link | -           | Yes                   |
| Max participants    | 2           | 5                     |
| Session length      | 10 minutes  | 30 minutes            |
| Sign-in options     | -           | Email, Google, Microsoft |


Visit [my.bigbluebutton.com](https://my.bigbluebutton.com/) to try it out.
