If you have upgrade to BigBlueButton Enhanced from Blindside Networks, you have a Guest Invite feature in BigBlueButton.

## Inviting a guest into a session

When you join a BigBlueButton session, the Session Details window will automatically appear.

![](/images/40060595798029.png)

Note: You can show the Session Details by selecting the **Meeting Title [1].**

If you are logged into BigBlueButton session as Moderator, the guest invite option is available.  Select **Invite a guest to join this meeting [2]**which opens a new tab with the following text.

![](/images/40060606540429.png)

Copy the **invite link [1]** to your clipboard.  You can then email it to a guest for them to join directly into your meeting.

By default, this link is only valid for the current active session.  If you want the guest invite link to be valid for the current session *and all future sessions* for this meeting, contact Blindside Networks.

## Joining a via a guest invite link

When opening a guest invite link, your browser will display the **Join a running meeting** prompt.

![](/images/40060573459469.png)

To join the session, enter your **name [1]** and click **Join [2]**.  You'll join the BigBlueButton session as viewer with the prefix (guest).

![](/images/40060573460109.png)
