---
title: "How do I invite a guest to a live meeting?"
description: "Moderators on BigBlueButton Enhanced can invite a guest into the active session by sharing a per-session invite link."
---

If your account has been upgraded to BigBlueButton Enhanced from Blindside Networks, moderators can invite a guest into a live session using a per-session invite link.

## Invite a guest

When you join a BigBlueButton session, the **Session Details** window appears automatically.

![Session Details window showing the meeting title and Invite a guest option](/images/40060595798029.png)

<Tip>
  To re-open the **Session Details** window later, select the **Meeting Title** **[1]**.
</Tip>

If you joined as a moderator, the guest invite option is available. Select **Invite a guest to join this meeting** **[2]** to open a new tab containing the invite link.

![Guest invite tab showing the invite link to copy and share](/images/40060606540429.png)

Copy the **invite link** **[1]** and email it to your guest so they can join directly.

<Note>
  By default, the invite link is only valid for the current active session. To make the link valid for the current session **and** all future sessions for this meeting, contact Blindside Networks.
</Note>

## Join a session via a guest invite link

When a guest opens the invite link, the **Join a running meeting** prompt appears.

![Join a running meeting prompt with name field and Join button](/images/40060573459469.png)

To join, enter your **name** **[1]** and select **Join** **[2]**. The guest enters the session as a viewer with the **(guest)** prefix on their name.

![Guest joined the BigBlueButton session as a viewer with the (guest) prefix on their name](/images/40060573460109.png)
