Blindside Networks focuses on the needs of educational institutions running online classes. This page lists common feature requests we have received, all subject to change based on customer demand.

## Auto closed captioning

BigBlueButton supports live closed captioning with the assistance of a stenographer. The captions appear in the recordings as subtitles.

If you use Instructure Canvas with Studio (which supports automatic captions), we can integrate your BigBlueButton recordings with Studio. Recordings appear automatically as shared recordings with the instructor, and you can use Studio's built-in captioning.

Today, most schools that need captions [download their recordings](/help/premium/how-do-i-download-a-recording) and upload them to a third-party captioning service.

Blindside Networks plans automatic captioning via third-party commercial captioning services (TBD).
