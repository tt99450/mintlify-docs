---
title: "Options for giving students visibility of other students' webcams"
description: "Some BigBlueButton Essentials accounts hide other students' webcams from students. To make all webcams visible, the instructor can share their screen or upgrade to BigBlueButton Enhanced."
---

On some BigBlueButton Essentials accounts, students cannot see other students' webcams in regular sessions. Webcams still play a major role in the lecture in those configurations:

1. Students can share their own webcams.
2. Educators can see every student's webcam.
3. Students can see the instructor's webcam.
4. Students can see each other's webcams in breakout rooms.

To make every webcam visible to every student — important in K12 — the instructor can share their screen while viewing the webcam strip. Students see the screen share and, in turn, see all the webcams from the instructor's view.

For a walkthrough, see [Showing webcams through screen sharing](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hG-F3nOhdnU&feature=youtu.be).

To enable cross-student webcam visibility directly, contact your LMS administrator about upgrading to BigBlueButton Enhanced.
