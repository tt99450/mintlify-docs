Blindside Networks offers  for BigBlueButton with the features described on this page. If you use the Free Tier built into your LMS, these features become available when you upgrade.

To upgrade, contact your LMS provider — Instructure, Schoology, Jenzabar, or your Moodle Partner — who resells Premium Tier hosting directly.

## Enhanced recordings

### Recordings as video files

Recordings are produced as a single video file containing all session content except chat (displayed beside the video during playback) and videos shared via **Share video**.

### Recordings are permanent

Recordings do not expire. They persist until you delete them.

### Recordings are downloadable

To [download a recording](/help/premium/how-do-i-download-a-recording), right-click within the playback area and select **Save video as…**.

### Recordings are protected (optional)

When recording links are passed to your LMS, our servers can generate temporary links that are only viewable by a single person — preventing students from copying and sharing recordings.

### Integration with Canvas Studio

If you have Canvas Studio, we can configure your account to [automatically upload recordings into Studio and share them with the instructor's account](/integrations/canvas/faq-integration-with-canvas-studio).

## Meeting analytics

For each recorded session, instructors see a **statistics** playback format that answers:

- Who attended?
- How long did they attend?
- What activities did they participate in (talking, chatting, raising hand, emojis)?
- How did they respond to polling?

All data is downloadable as CSV. See the [statistics recording format](https://blindsidenetworks.com/statistics). All times in the statistics reports are in UTC.

## Personal rooms

If you authenticate instructor email accounts via Office 365 or Google Apps, we can provide an interface outside your LMS to create rooms, invite users, and manage recordings. Personal rooms have permanent URLs.

See the [Personal Rooms admin guide](/help/personal-rooms/admin/site-settings).

## Guest invites

Moderators can [invite guests](/help/premium/how-do-i-invite-a-guest-to-a-live-meeting) into a live session.

## Dial-in number

Every Premium Tier account in North America includes a default dial-in number. For Canada, Europe, and Asia/Pacific, we can provide a dial-in number for an additional cost.

## Account customization

We can configure account-specific [Premium Tier customizations](/help/premium/premium-tier-customizations), including:

- Welcome message
- Show/hide dial-in number to users
- Default lock settings
- Sessions longer than 540 minutes

## Frequently asked questions

### How is the cost of Premium Tier calculated?

Premium Tier costs are based on the maximum number of concurrent sessions (or, in some K12 cases, concurrent users) running at any one time. A concurrent session is any BigBlueButton session with two or more users that runs for more than 15 minutes.

In practice, you have multiple sessions throughout the day. The more that run at the same time, the more capacity we provision — so we charge based on concurrency.

A concurrent session is not tied to a specific user. There is no second login layer. Instructors and students join a BigBlueButton session by selecting a link in the LMS, and the session opens in their browser.

For pricing details, contact your LMS provider or [reach out to us directly](https://blindsidenetworks.com/contact/).

### Can I track my concurrent sessions?

Yes. We can provide a URL that displays a graph of your peak concurrent sessions.

### What happens if I exceed the number of concurrent sessions?

BigBlueButton continues to work — there is no hard-coded limit on concurrent sessions. However, if your usage is significantly above the number of purchased concurrent sessions, we will reach out to discuss adding capacity.
