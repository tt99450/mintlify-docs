---
title: "Start and stop recording"
description: "Moderators use the Start/Stop Recording button to capture the session when recording is enabled for the meeting."
---

When a BigBlueButton meeting is enabled for recording, the moderator uses the **Start/Stop Recording** button in the live session to [start](#start-the-recording) or [stop](#stop-or-pause-the-recording) the recording.

<Note>
  Enabling recording when the meeting is created does not auto-capture the session. You must use the **Start/Stop Recording** button in the live session for a recording to be produced.
</Note>

The recording icon sits in the top centre of the BigBlueButton interface, to the right of the session title.

## Start the recording

To start the recording, select the **Start Recording** button.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 24 At 10 49 30 AM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-24at10.49.30AM.png)
</Frame>

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 24 At 10 45 53 AM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-24at10.45.53AM.png)
</Frame>

The recording icon turns red and a timer counts up the recorded duration.

## Stop or pause the recording

To stop or pause the recording, select the **red icon and timer**.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 24 At 10 55 17 AM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-24at10.55.17AM.png)
</Frame>

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 24 At 10 46 45 AM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-24at10.46.45AM.png)
</Frame>

<Note>
  Recording processing takes about as long as the **full** session duration and only begins after the session ends or all attendees log out.
</Note>

<Note>
  All sessions have a maximum duration of 9 hours.
</Note>
