---
title: "Give/take presenter role"
description: "How moderators assign or reclaim the presenter role from any user in the session."
---

Moderators can assign the presenter role to any user in the session, including themselves. The presenter can [upload a presentation](/help/presenter-guide/upload-a-presentation-enable-for-download), share a [link from YouTube](/help/presenter-guide/share-an-external-audiovideo-link), or [engage a poll](/help/presenter-guide/use-pollingquzzing).

By default, the first moderator to join the session is the presenter.

</Frame>

## Make a user the presenter

To assign the presenter role to another user, select the user's name in the **Users list** **[1]**, then select **Make presenter** **[2]**.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 16 At 8 06 06 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-16at8.06.06PM.png)
</Frame>

The presenter icon moves to the new presenter's avatar in the **Users list**, and that user gains presenter controls in the presentation area.

## Reclaim presenter status

To take back the presenter role, select your own name in the **Users list** and select **Take presenter role**.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 16 At 8 08 28 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-16at8.08.28PM.png)
</Frame>

You can also reclaim presenter status from the action button in the presentation area. Select the action button, then select **Take presenter role**.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 16 At 8 04 59 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-16at8.04.59PM.png)
</Frame>

The presenter icon moves to your avatar in the **Users list**, and you regain access to the presenter tools.

## Request the presenter role as a viewer

If you are not a moderator, ask a moderator in the session to make you the presenter.
