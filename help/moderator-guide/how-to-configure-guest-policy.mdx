---
title: "How to configure Guest Policy"
description: "Moderators set the guest policy to control whether users can join the session, are denied automatically, or wait in a lobby for approval."
---

Moderators set the **guest policy** to control how new users are admitted to the session — automatically allowed, automatically denied, or queued in a lobby for moderator approval.

## Set the guest policy

To set the policy, select the gear icon next to **USERS**, then select **Guest policy**.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 16 At 9 04 06 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-16at9.04.06PM.png)
</Frame>

Choose one of three options:

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 16 At 8 54 06 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-16at8.54.06PM.png)
</Frame>

- **Always deny** — every user attempting to join is denied entry automatically.
- **Always accept** — every user attempting to join is allowed in automatically.
- **Ask moderator** — joining users wait in a lobby. Moderators can allow or deny each request individually, or handle every waiting user in one action.

## Manage users in the lobby

When **Ask moderator** is set, moderators see pending requests and can act on them.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 16 At 8 55 20 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-16at8.55.20PM.png)
</Frame>

You have three actions per guest:

- **Allow** — let the guest join the session.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 16 At 8 55 33 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-16at8.55.33PM-1.png)
</Frame>

- **Message** — send a note to a guest waiting in the lobby.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 16 At 8 55 52 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-16at8.55.52PM.png)
</Frame>

- **Deny** — block the guest from entering.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 16 At 8 56 05 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-16at8.56.05PM.png)
</Frame>

## What guests see in the lobby

Guests see a lobby screen and cannot view the session until a moderator admits them.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 16 At 8 55 07 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-16at8.55.07PM.png)
</Frame>

Messages sent to the **Guest Lobby** appear on the same waiting screen.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 16 At 9 07 07 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-16at9.07.07PM.png)
</Frame>
