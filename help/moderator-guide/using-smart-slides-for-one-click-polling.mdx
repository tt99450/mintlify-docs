---
title: "Using Smart Slides for one-click polling"
description: "BigBlueButton automatically extracts poll/quiz questions (and video links) from your uploaded slides."
---

BigBlueButton automatically extracts poll/quiz questions (and video links) from your uploaded slides. We call it **Smart Slides**. 

When you have a slide deck with poll-type questions, you'll see a button to automatically start a poll.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 24 At 1 03 27 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-24at1.03.27PM.png)
</Frame>

You can find a \*\*Quick Poll \*\*button in the bottom left-hand corner of the toolbar to start your poll or quiz.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 24 At 1 06 35 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-24at1.06.35PM.png)
</Frame>

The poll will appear with the information extracted from the slide:

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 24 At 1 10 17 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-24at1.10.17PM.png)
</Frame>

And appear to users when you start the poll or quiz:

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 24 At 1 13 24 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-24at1.13.24PM.png)
</Frame>

To start a quiz, add two underscore "\_\_" characters next to the correct answer on your slide.

Make sure the underscore characters are the same colour as your presentation background, so students cannot see it.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 24 At 1 18 03 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-24at1.18.03PM.png)
</Frame>

This will automatically create a quiz with the correct answer in place.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 24 At 12 58 14 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-24at12.58.14PM.png)
</Frame>

If you add an external video link to your slides, you can select the **external video button**, found in the bottom-left corner of the toolbar. This will play the video to everyone in the session.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 24 At 12 56 13 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-24at12.56.13PM-1.png)
</Frame>
