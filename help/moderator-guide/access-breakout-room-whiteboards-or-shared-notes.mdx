---
title: "Access breakout room whiteboards or shared notes"
---

When creating breakout rooms, you can select **Save whiteboard** or **Save shared notes**.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 24 At 11 52 13 AM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-24at11.52.13AM.png)
</Frame>

This will export whiteboard presentations or shared notes from all of the breakout rooms you created as PDFs. When breakout rooms end, you can access the files from the **Action menu \[\+\]**.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 24 At 11 49 11 AM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-24at11.49.11AM.png)
</Frame>

Or, you can access the files from the **Upload/Manage presentations** menu.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 24 At 11 47 00 AM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-24at11.47.00AM-1.png)
</Frame>

Breakout room presentations and shared notes can be managed as shown in the guides below:

- [Upload a presentation and enable for download](/help/presenter-guide/upload-a-presentation-enable-for-download)
- [Shared notes as a presenter](/help/presenter-guide/shared-notes-presenter)
