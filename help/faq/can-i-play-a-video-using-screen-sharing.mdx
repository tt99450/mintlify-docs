---
title: "Can I play a video using screen sharing?"
description: "Share videos in BigBlueButton using the built-in external video option or screen sharing."
---

The best way to share a YouTube, Vimeo, or Canvas Studio video is through the built-in [Share external video](/help/presenter-guide/share-an-external-audiovideo-link) option. This instructs each user's browser to load the video directly.

However, if you want to use [Screen sharing](/help/presenter-guide/share-my-screen) to share a video (start screen sharing and then play the video on your screen outside of BigBlueButton), we recommend using the Chrome browser.

When using Chrome, enable **Also share system audio** so Chrome shares audio with users. Once enabled, audio from the screen plays for everyone in the session.

![Image](/images/image-1.png)

<Warning>
  Share external video is the most efficient, but the video won't appear in the recording as the user's browser directly plays the video (it does not go through the server).
</Warning>
