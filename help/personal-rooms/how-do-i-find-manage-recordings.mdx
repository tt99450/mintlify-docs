You can view and manage Personal Rooms recordings two ways: per room ([Room recordings](#recordings-for-the-selected-room)) or across your account ([All recordings](#all-recordings)).

## Recordings for the selected room

When you log in, the currently selected room is your **Home Room**. The bottom panel lists all recordings for the selected room.

![Recordings tab listing recordings for the selected room with name, date, length, attendees, visibility, and format columns](/images/21759094485133.png)

| # | Column |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | **Recordings tab** — shows all recordings for this room. |
| 2 | Search for a recording. |
| 3 | Recording name (matches the room name) and the date it was recorded. |
| 4 | Length in minutes. |
| 5 | Number of attendees in the session. |
| 6 | Visibility of the recording. |
| 7 | Available formats for the recording. |
| 8 | Quickly copy the recording link. |
| 9 | Quickly delete the recording. |

### Recording visibility

By default, recordings are **unlisted** — only the room owner can access them. To allow attendees with the room link to access a recording, change visibility from the dropdown.

![Recording visibility dropdown showing Public, Protected, Published, and Unpublished options](/images/21759094498829.png)

- **Public** — appears on the Public Recordings page on the Room Join page. The link can be shared with anyone.
- **Protected** — must be viewed from within Personal Rooms.
- **Published** — accessible to anyone who has the link.
- **Unpublished** — not accessible to anyone.

<Note>
  Setting a recording to **Public** also makes the meeting statistics available.

  An **Unpublished** recording can still be viewed by anyone given the playback URL directly, even without access to the room link.
</Note>

### Recording formats

Up to four formats are produced for each processed recording. Selecting **presentation** or **video** opens the playback in a new tab. Selecting **statistics** or **notes** also opens in a new tab.

Anyone with access to the room can view the available recording formats. The **statistics** format provides an overview of attendance, participation, and engagement.

<Note>
  If you use Blindside Networks hosting, deleted recordings can be recovered up to 14 days after the **live** session.
</Note>

## All recordings

To view every recording across your account, select the home icon, then the **Recordings** tab.

<img src="/images/21823501648269.png" alt="Home icon" />

![Recordings tab on the home view showing all recordings across the account](/images/21823493786765.png)

You can also select **Recordings** from the navigation toolbar in the upper-right corner of the interface.

![Recordings link in the navigation toolbar](/images/360034095012.png)

The **All recordings** view lists every recording in chronological order.
