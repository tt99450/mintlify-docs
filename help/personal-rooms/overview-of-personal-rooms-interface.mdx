After you sign in to Personal Rooms, the main page displays four key areas: **Account Information**, the **Home Button**, the **Personal Room Manager**, and the **Recordings Manager**.

![Personal Rooms main page with Account Information, Home Button, Personal Room Manager, and Recordings Manager areas highlighted](/images/21750869642637.png)

- **Account Information** **[1]** — update your profile, access the help center, and (if applicable) open the Administrator Panel.
- **Home Button** **[2]** — quickly return to the home page that lists all of your Personal Rooms.
- **Personal Room Manager** **[3]** — shows the room title and lets you start, join, or copy the room link, alongside last-session information.
- **Recordings Manager** **[4]** — shows recordings, meeting statistics, and the invite link for the selected room. Processed recordings appear here once they are available.

## Recordings Manager

![Recordings Manager listing recordings with name, date, length, attendees, visibility, and format columns](/images/21750947637645.png)

| # | Column |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | **Recordings tab** — shows all recordings for this room. |
| 2 | Search for a recording. |
| 3 | Recording name and the date and time of the session. |
| 4 | Length of the recording in minutes. |
| 5 | Number of users who attended. |
| 6 | Visibility of the recording. |
| 7 | Available formats. |
| 8 | Quickly copy the recording link. |
| 9 | Quickly delete the recording. |
