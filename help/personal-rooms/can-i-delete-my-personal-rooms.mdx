---
title: "Can I delete my personal rooms?"
description: "Yes — room owners can delete a personal room from the Room Menu, which also deletes every recording associated with that room."
---

Yes. As the owner of a Personal Room, you can delete it at any time.

<Warning>
  Deleting a personal room also deletes every recording associated with that room. The action cannot be undone.
</Warning>

For example, here is a room called "Welcome to Greenlight".

![Personal room titled Welcome to Greenlight](/images/Screen_Shot_2019-07-25_at_3.46.35_PM.png)

To delete the room, select the **Room Menu** **[1]** and select **Delete** **[2]**. A confirmation dialog appears.

![Delete confirmation dialog for a personal room](/images/Screen_Shot_2019-07-24_at_1.20.24_PM.png)

Selecting **I'm sure, delete this room** removes the room and all of its recordings.
