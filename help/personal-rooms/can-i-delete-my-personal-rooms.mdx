## Yes!Â As the owner of the page you can always delete a personal room.

**_\*\*It is important to note that deleting a personal room will also delete any / all recordings associated with that room\*\*_**

For example, here is a room called "Welcome to Greenlight".

![Screen_Shot_2019-07-25_at_3.46.35_PM.png](/images/Screen_Shot_2019-07-25_at_3.46.35_PM.png)

To delete this room, select the **Room Menu [1] **and choose **Delete [2]. **You'll see a confirmation dialog to confirm you want to delete this room.

![Screen_Shot_2019-07-24_at_1.20.24_PM.png](/images/Screen_Shot_2019-07-24_at_1.20.24_PM.png)

Clicking "I'm sure, delete this room" will delete the room and all its associated recordings.
