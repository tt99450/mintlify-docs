## Yes! When you create a room you have the ability to invite attendees.

![Screen_Shot_2019-07-25_at_2.21.37_PM.png](/images/360034068871.png)

The first way to invite attendees is through the **Invite Participants [1]** link provided before entering your session. Simply copy the link and send out via email or direct message. If participants are trying to join a meeting with an invite link and the meeting has not been started they will see the following message displayed:

![Screen_Shot_2019-07-25_at_2.26.07_PM.png](/images/360034069051.png)

You may notice that when the session is running, you will see the **Personal Room Invite Link [2]** in the welcome message for the chat.
