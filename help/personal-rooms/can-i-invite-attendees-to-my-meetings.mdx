---
title: "Can I invite attendees to my meetings?"
description: "Yes — Personal Rooms owners can invite attendees using the Invite Participants link or share the in-session invite from the chat."
---

Yes. As the owner of a Personal Room, you can invite attendees in two ways: using the [**Invite Participants**](#invite-with-the-invite-participants-link) link before the session, or sharing the [**Personal Room Invite Link**](#share-the-in-session-invite-link) once the session is running.

## Invite with the Invite Participants link

![Personal Room dashboard showing the Invite Participants link](/images/360034068871.png)

To invite attendees before the session starts, copy the **Invite Participants** **[1]** link and send it via email or message.

If a participant opens the invite link before you have started the session, they see a waiting message.

![Meeting hasn't started yet message](/images/360034069051.png)

When you, the owner, enter the room and start the BigBlueButton session, anyone on the waiting page is joined into the session automatically.

## Share the in-session invite link

When the session is running, the **Personal Room Invite Link** **[2]** appears in the welcome message in the chat.

![Personal Room Invite Link displayed in the chat welcome message](/images/360034085092.png)
