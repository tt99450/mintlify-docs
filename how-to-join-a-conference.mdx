---
title: "How to Join a Conference as a User"
---

To join a Conference in Schoology, select the **Conferences \[1\]** panel in the navigation menu.

<Frame>
  ![Joinuserconf](/images/joinuserconf.png)
</Frame>

Select the **link \[2\]** of the Conference in progress to join the session. You will be taken to the BigBlueButton session in a new tab.
