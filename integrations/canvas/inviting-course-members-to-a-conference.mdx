## You can add any user in your course to your conference.

By default, all members in the course will be invited to the conference.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 31 At 8 29 59 AM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-31at8.29.59AM.png)
</Frame>

To invite specific individuals registered to the course to a Conference, un-check the **Invite All Course Members **option then select the checkbox(es) for the individual member(s) you would like to invite.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 31 At 8 50 32 AM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-31at8.50.32AM.png)
</Frame>

### \*\*Only members invited to a Conference will see the associated recordings, however, members who are invited do not need to attend the live session to see the associated recordings\*\*
