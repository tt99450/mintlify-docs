## If you enabled recording when you created the conference and engaged the start/stop record button in the live session, you will have access to a recording.

To locate a recording select **BigBlueButton** in the course navigation.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 31 At 8 58 39 AM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-31at8.58.39AM.png)
</Frame>

Under **Concluded Conferences**locate the conference activity to view available recordings.

<Frame>
  ![Confdelete](/images/confdelete.png)
</Frame>

You may need to expand the **Concluded Conference Activity Menu \[1\]** to access the recording **Video Link \[4\]**. You can also** Delete \[5\] **a recording.

If you are a premium tier Canvas Customer you will also have access to recorded meeting **Statistics \[2\] **and** Notes \[3\]**.

### It is important to note that it takes approximately the same amount of time for the recording to process as the duration of the live session.
