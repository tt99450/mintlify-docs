In Canvas BigBlueButton Conferences, when a conference has a recording you will see a small triangle to the left of the conference title.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 31 At 8 55 54 AM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-31at8.55.54AM.png)
</Frame>

Clicking the triangle will expand the conference to show a list of all available recordings.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 31 At 8 53 32 AM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-31at8.53.32AM.png)
</Frame>

Clicking on **video** will then open the recording and play it in a new window.  (You may see different recording formats in your instance of Canvas).
