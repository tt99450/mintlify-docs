In Canvas BigBlueButton Conferences, when a conference has a recording you will see a small triangle to the left of the conference title.

![mceclip1.png](/images/mceclip1_7.png)

Clicking the triangle will expand the conference to show a list of all available recordings.

![mceclip0.png](/images/mceclip0_8.png)

Clicking on **video** will then open the recording and play it in a new window.Â  (You may see different recording formats in your instance of Canvas).
