## You can delete new and concluded conferences.

Deleting a concluded conference also immediately removes the conference from the server, in addition to all associated recordings.

### To delete a conference

![Screen_Shot_2022-10-14_at_10.57.44_AM.png](/images/9885753257613)

Open **BigBlueButton** in course navigation.

![Screen_Shot_2018-09-26_at_3.22.20_PM.png](/images/Screen_Shot_2018-09-26_at_3.22.20_PM.png)

Locate the conference you would like to delete and select the **Settings Icon,**and then click **Delete**.

### Deleting a conference that is "in progress" will end the meeting and remove it and any associated recordings from the servers.
