---
title: "Roles and capabilities"
description: "Understand which Moodle roles and permissions affect who can create, join, manage, and administer BigBlueButton activities."
---

BigBlueButton behavior in Moodle depends on both the plugin’s own capabilities and the broader Moodle role model.

## Role-sensitive areas

Permissions affect who can:

- create activities
- join rooms
- join as moderator
- manage recordings
- see hidden activities
- change advanced activity settings

## Practical admin guidance

Review capabilities carefully for:

- managers
- editing teachers
- non-editing teachers
- students
- guests

If teachers report missing controls, this page is one of the first places to audit.
