---
title: "Recording defaults"
description: "Set the default recording behavior for BigBlueButton activities on your Moodle site."
---

BigBlueButton activities can be enabled to provide recording during live sessions. Administrators can control the defaults that teachers see when they create new activities.

## Common defaults

Recording defaults may include:

- whether sessions can be recorded by default
- how recordings are displayed in the activity
- whether recordings from deleted activities are included
- whether imported recordings are shown

## Why this matters

These defaults shape the teacher experience and reduce the need to configure the same recording behavior repeatedly in every course.
