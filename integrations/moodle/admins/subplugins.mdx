title: "Subplugins"
description: "Understand how BigBlueButton extension subplugins are surfaced in Moodle and how they can expand the integration."
---

Some Moodle sites include BigBlueButton extension subplugins that add extra features on top of the core activity.

## What subplugins can change

Depending on the site, subplugins may add:

- extra room settings
- enhanced recording workflows
- analytics and reporting
- guest approval flows
- presentation enhancements
- custom integrations

## Admin responsibility

Administrators should document which extensions are installed on their site because they can change both the teacher workflow and the user interface.

## Best practice

When troubleshooting, always confirm whether a behavior comes from:

- the core BigBlueButton Moodle activity
- a site-specific extension or subplugin
