BigBlueButton activity completion in Moodle depends on event callback data from the BigBlueButton server.

## Common completion models

Sites may use completion rules based on:

- attendance duration
- live participation
- other configured Moodle completion conditions

For setup guidance, see [Activity completion](/integrations/moodle/admins/activity-completion).
