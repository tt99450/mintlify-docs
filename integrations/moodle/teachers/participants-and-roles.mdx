---
title: "Participants and roles"
description: "Customize which users enter a BigBlueButton activity and whether they join as viewers or moderators."
---

BigBlueButton activities in Moodle let you customize the participant list beyond the default behavior.

## Default behavior

By default:

- all enrolled course users enter as **viewers**
- the owner of the activity enters as a **moderator**

![Default participant mapping for a BigBlueButton activity](/images/Screen_Shot_2018-10-12_at_9.41.18_AM.png)

## Add participants

You can add participants based on:

- a Moodle role
- an individual enrolled user

When adding users manually, only users enrolled in the course will appear in the list.

![Choose role-based participants from the participant controls](/images/Screen_Shot_2018-10-12_at_11.44.58_AM.png)

![Select a specific Moodle role and add it to the mapping](/images/Screen_Shot_2018-10-12_at_11.50.37_AM.png)

## Change entry role

For each participant mapping, you can choose whether the user or role enters as:

- **Viewer**
- **Moderator**

Moderators have more controls in the live BigBlueButton session than viewers.

![Participants appear in the mapping list after being added](/images/Screen_Shot_2018-10-12_at_12.24.17_PM.png)

![Choose whether a participant enters as viewer or moderator](/images/Screen_Shot_2018-10-12_at_12.40.24_PM.png)

## Remove participants

To remove an added participant mapping, use the delete or trash icon in the participant list.

## Important note

Role-based behavior still depends on how Moodle roles and permissions are configured on your site.
