---

If your site is missing translated BigBlueButton strings or uses outdated wording, the fix usually follows Moodle’s normal localization workflow.

## Start here

- for standard language packs, go to [Language customization](/integrations/moodle/admins/language-customization)
- for custom wording, use Moodle’s language customization tools

## Typical problems

- the language pack has not been updated
- the site installed new BigBlueButton strings after the language pack was last refreshed
- local customizations are overriding expected wording
