## Any administrator or owner of a BigBlueButtonBN room/activity can control who can and cannot view recordings.

Once a recording has processed and is available for viewing, its visibility and access permissions can be customized.

![Screen_Shot_2018-10-11_at_3.13.39_PM.png](/images/360013198752)

Locate the recording you would like to edit. Under the **toolbar**column are three available options.

![Screen_Shot_2018-10-11_at_3.53.28_PM.png](/images/360013207731)

To make a recording **private**select the **lock icon.** When locked, a recording will only be visible in Moodle.  The link to the recording cannot be shared outside of Moodle.

**![Screen_Shot_2018-10-11_at_3.53.32_PM.png](/images/360013207751)**

Selecting the eye icon will**unpublish/publish**the recording link.

When a recording is unpublished it means that even if someone bookmarks the link when viewing a recording, that bookmarked link will no longer be able to access the recording for viewing.

**![Screen_Shot_2018-10-11_at_3.49.27_PM.png](/images/360013198912)**

To **Delete**a recording select the **trash can** icon. Deleting a recording will delete the viewing links and statistics report tied to the recording.
