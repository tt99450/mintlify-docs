Once a BigBlueButton activity has been added to a course, joining the live room is simple.

## Join a session

1. Open the course.
2. Select the BigBlueButton activity.
3. Review the session status on the activity page.
4. Select **Join session** when it becomes available.

The session opens in a separate browser tab or window.

## What you may see before joining

Depending on the settings, Moodle may show that:

- the room is ready
- the session has not started yet
- you are waiting for a moderator
- the session is closed
- the session is full

If the activity is scheduled, the join option may not appear until the session opens.

## When groups are enabled

If the course uses groups, the join flow can behave differently:

- some users will be sent directly to their group room
- some users will be able to choose from one or more rooms
- teachers may see more room options than students

See [Groups and grouping](/integrations/moodle/teachers/groups-and-grouping).
