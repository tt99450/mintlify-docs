---
title: "Find and play recordings"
description: "Find processed BigBlueButton recordings in Moodle and play them back using the available recording formats."
---

If recording is enabled for a BigBlueButton activity, processed recordings appear below the activity after the session concludes.

## Find recordings

1. Open the course.
2. Select the BigBlueButton activity.
3. Scroll to the recordings section below the room details.

Recordings normally appear in chronological order.

![Processed recordings appear below the activity](/images/Screen_Shot_2018-10-10_at_12.05.11_PM.png)

![Open the activity to access its recordings](/images/Screen_Shot_2018-10-10_at_12.05.15_PM.png)

## Play a recording

Look in the playback column and choose one of the available formats, such as:

- **Presentation**
- **Video**

The recording opens in a separate browser tab.

![Choose a playback format such as Presentation or Video](/images/Screen_Shot_2018-10-10_at_12.10.51_PM.png)

![Recording playback opens in a separate tab](/images/Screen_Shot_2018-10-10_at_12.13.59_PM.png)

## Download behavior

Some playback formats may also allow download or saving through the browser, depending on the format and the user’s permissions.
