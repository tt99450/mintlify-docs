Use BigBlueButton activities to host virtual lectures, hold office hours, and coordinate student groups from inside Moodle.

## Before you begin

Make sure:

- your Moodle administrator has installed and configured the BigBlueButton activity module
- you have permission to add activities to the course

If you cannot create a BigBlueButton activity, your institution has likely restricted that feature. Contact your Moodle administrator.

## Create the activity

1. Open the Moodle course where you want to add the activity.
2. Turn editing on.
3. Select **Add an activity or resource** in the topic or week where you want the room.
4. Choose **BigBlueButton**.
5. Enter a room name.
6. Optionally add a description that appears on the course page.
7. Review any room, recording, participant, and schedule settings you want to change.
8. Save the activity.

## What to configure first

For a good first setup, review these areas:

- [Room settings](/integrations/moodle/teachers/room-settings)
- [Schedule a session](/integrations/moodle/teachers/schedule-session)
- [Recording settings](/integrations/moodle/teachers/recording-settings)

## General setup fields

The activity form usually starts with:

- an **Instance Type** selector
- a room or activity name
- optional description and notification settings

## Save options

When you finish, you can usually choose:

- **Save and return to course**
- **Save and display**

If you save and display, Moodle takes you directly to the activity page where you can launch the session.
